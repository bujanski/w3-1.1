Banana Facts

Welcome to Banana Facts, the definitive online source for facts about bananas. All facts listed here are thoroughly researched and vetted for accuracy by the Academy of Banana Science.
Whether you're a fledgling banana enthusiast or an established expert in the field, Banana Facts has your back!™



Bananas as a food source

Bananas are a superfood. They provide all the calories and nutrients necessary to keep a person happy and healthy.
Did you know: A single banana can feed a family of five for a week.




Banana for scale

Bananas are the official ISO standard of measurement for all size comparisons on the internet.




Freckles

Many young bananas develop freckles after spending time in the sun.




Banana myth

Many people mistakenly believe bananas are a fruit. Bananas are actually simple animals that reproduce by parthenogenesis.




How to grade your bananas

The Academy of Banana Science (ABS) has developed a grading system to categorize bananas based on their degree of perfection.

Grade Description
Ultra super mega fine The highest grade of banana. Well shaped and entirely free of blemishes. Only seven bananas have received this grade.
Ultra fine The best banana you're likely to see in your lifetime. Really stellar.
Super fine Hey, now that's a good-looking banana.
Fine A perfectly fine banana.
